The History of Websites

The Internet is a integral component of life for people around the world.

Since January 1, 1983 the Internet; a global computer network, has provided a variety of information and communication facilities, consisting of interconnected networks using standardized communication protocol via the World Wide Web. The World Wide Web is an information system on the internet which allows documents to be connected to other documents by hypertext links, enabling the user to search for information by moving from one document to another.
